Duties:

                        
    Harvest and inspect crops by hand:
                            1. Irrigate farm soil and maintain ditches or pipes and pumps
                            2. Operate and service farm machinery and tools
                            3. Spray fertilizer or pesticide solutions to control insects, fungi, and weeds
                            4. Move shrubs, plants, and trees with wheelbarrows or tractors
                            5. Feed livestock and clean and disinfect their pens, cages, yards, and hutches
                            6. Examine animals to detect symptoms of illnesses or injuries and administer vaccines to protect animals from diseases
                            7. Use brands, tags, or tattoos to mark livestock in order to identify ownership and grade
                            8. Herd livestock to pastures for grazing or to scales, trucks, or other enclosures
